About
Did you notice how noisy or quiet your coffee was?

Taken from a recording of a coffee shop, based on the amount of caffeine that coffee brands put into their coffee, the more noisy the coffee shop gets.
For example, McDonald's has the lowest caffeine input in their coffee, so their coffee is quieter compared to Starbucks, who has the highest amount of caffeine.
This site was developed to create a more interactive awareness to the amount of caffeine put into our bodies, based on the brand of coffee that you drink on a regular basis.
Some people have no idea about the amount of caffeine they are putting into their bodies so this site is to inform and change the way you drink coffee.
